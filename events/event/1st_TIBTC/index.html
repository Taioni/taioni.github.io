1st TIBTC

What This Competition???

There was the MBMSP International Blacking Technique Competition called MIBTC. However, it was only held three times and then it disappeared. Sad.
So I thought I would hold a competition similar to MIBTC to improve the technical skills of the Black MIDI community.
However, I thought that if I tried to recruit judges, the number of participants would decrease, so I tried to have the judges be the participants themselves. (That way I could participate myself (yay))

MIBTCなくなった。 → 大会無いのは哀しい。 → やるか～～～ → 開催!!!
審査員、集められるほど、人がいない。→ 参加者同士で審査すればいいじゃん。→ 解決!!!!!!!

Assignment song▼

uma vs. ガリガリさむし - To : Be Continued

Rules▼

01. Judges : All participants
02. Evaluation Content : 10 points each

  • ・Melody
  • ・Audio
  • ・Visuals
  • ・Transitions
  • ・Creativity
03. The main focus of the piece should be on the assigned songs
04. Arrangements must be up to 5 minutes long
05. Maximum number of notes : 20million
06. Do not make your information personally identifiable (For example, putting your name in the file name, putting your logo at the beginning of the MIDI, etc.)
07. No collaboration allowed
08. Each participant may enter up to one MIDI (The last MIDI entry will be used for evaluation.)
09. Nothing other than what each person has created may be made public
10. No part of the video may be leaked until the competition video is released
